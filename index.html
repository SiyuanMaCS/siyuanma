Siyuan Ma

PhD Student, CUHK

Email / Google Scholar / GitHub

I am an incoming PhD student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Weiyang Liu, and currently a research intern at ByteDance. Before joining CUHK, I received my B.S. from Peking University in 2025.

I also collaborate with Prof. Yandong Wen (Westlake University) and Prof. Chaowei Xiao (UW–Madison).

My research is on embodied AI, world models, and multimodal agents. I am especially interested in evaluating and improving how large multimodal models understand, predict, and act in the physical world. Previously I worked on multimodal LLM safety.

I am always happy to discuss research. Feel free to reach out by email.
